Organization Description:
Blue Bees Recovery is a nonprofit rehabilitation organization that uses beekeeping as a therapeutic tool to support recovering addicts and their children. Established on World Bee Day in 2022 by siblings Nuren and Melissa Mayer, the organization was inspired by their personal experiences with addiction and their belief in the restorative power of nature. Through hands-on beekeeping activities, participants develop patience, responsibility, mindfulness, and self-discipline while rebuilding confidence and self-worth. Children involved in the program benefit from a safe and supportive environment where they learn teamwork, environmental awareness, and valuable life skills. Supported by partners such as The Honey Company, Brother Bees, and Best Bees Company, Blue Bees Recovery has helped hundreds of individuals and families find stability, healing, and hope for a brighter future.

Reasons for creating the business
I chose to create an organization and named it Blue Bees because beekeeping offers a unique kind of therapy. Working with bees needs patience, care, and mindfulness. These qualities help recovering addicts’ discipline and self-worth. For children of addicts, I think beekeeping provides a safe, proper environment where they can learn to be responsible, work in a team, and provide environmental care.
Website Description
The Blue Bees Recovery website serves as an informational and community engagement platform for the organization. It provides visitors with insight into the organization’s mission, history, rehabilitation programs, and volunteer opportunities. The website features a clean and professional design that reflects the organization’s values of healing, growth, and environmental stewardship through the use of calming blue tones and nature-inspired imagery. Users can learn about the services offered, explore ways to get involved, and contact the organization for further information or support. Overall, the website acts as a digital gateway that connects individuals, families, volunteers, and sponsors with the work and impact of Blue Bees Recovery.
The primary target audience of the Blue Bees Recovery website includes individuals recovering from substance addiction and families seeking alternative rehabilitation and support services. The website also targets parents and guardians looking for programs that can positively impact both themselves and their children. A secondary audience consists of volunteers, sponsors, donors, and community members who are interested in supporting rehabilitation initiatives and environmental conservation efforts.

Webpages and Content
Home Webpage
The Home page serves as the main entry point to the Blue Bees Recovery website and introduces visitors to the organization’s mission. It features a clean and welcoming layout with dominant shades of blue, white, and light grey, creating a calm and professional appearance. The page contains a large hero image of a bee farm, a brief introduction to the organization, and cards highlighting volunteering opportunities and available programs. Interactive buttons encourage users to learn more about the organization and its services. This page is important because it creates the first impression of the website and guides visitors to explore the other sections.
About Webpage
The About page provides detailed information about Blue Bees Recovery, its background, and its mission. The page uses a structured layout with large images, informational sections, and blue accents that maintain consistency with the site's overall colour scheme. Content includes the organization’s goals, values, and the role that beekeeping plays in supporting rehabilitation and personal growth. Visual elements such as cards and images help present information in an engaging and organized manner. This page is important because it builds trust and helps visitors understand the purpose and impact of the organization.
Contact Webpage
The Contact page allows visitors to communicate directly with the organization and obtain important contact information. It features a simple and user-friendly layout with a contact form, contact details, and clearly organized information blocks. The blue colour scheme and clean design maintain consistency with the rest of the website while ensuring readability. Users can submit enquiries, request information, or seek assistance through the contact form. This page is important because it provides a direct communication channel between the organization and the public.
Programs & Services Page
The Programs & Services page explains the various support programs and activities offered by Blue Bees Recovery. Its layout consists of a prominent hero section followed by information cards that present each program in a clear and structured format. The dominant blue and white colour palette reinforces the organization’s branding while creating a professional appearance. The content describes rehabilitation programs, family support initiatives, and touring services available through the organization. This page is important because it informs potential participants, volunteers, and stakeholders about the specific services provided by Blue Bees Recovery.

Get Involved Page
The Get Involved page encourages community participation and volunteer engagement within Blue Bees Recovery. It includes a large hero image, informative content sections, and a volunteer registration form designed to collect user information. The page follows the website's blue-and-white theme and uses spacious layouts to create an inviting and accessible experience. Visitors can learn about volunteer opportunities and sign up to contribute to the organization’s activities. This page is important because it helps grow community involvement and supports the organization’s mission through active participation.
